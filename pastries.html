Kouign-Amann

Originating from Brittany, France, the Kouign-Amann is a heavenly pastry that is sure to captivate your taste buds. Its name translates to "butter cake" in Breton, and it lives up to its reputation. Made with layers of buttery croissant-like dough and generously sprinkled with sugar, the Kouign-Amann is baked to perfection. The result is a caramelized and flaky exterior with a tender and moist interior. With each bite, you'll experience the perfect balance of sweetness and buttery goodness. Indulge in this delectable treat for a truly unforgettable pastry experience.
