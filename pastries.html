Cinnamon rolls are sweet pastries with a rich, buttery dough swirled with cinnamon and sugar filling. They are baked until golden brown and often drizzled with cream cheese icing or glaze. The warm and aromatic flavors of cinnamon make these rolls a popular choice for breakfast or a sweet treat.

Eclair

Eclairs are elegant pastries made from choux pastry dough, filled with creamy custard or whipped cream, and topped with a layer of glossy chocolate icing. The light and airy texture of the pastry, combined with the luscious filling and decadent chocolate topping, creates a delightful dessert experience.

Kouign-Amann

Kouign-Amann is a traditional Breton pastry that originated in France. It consists of a flaky and buttery dough that is folded and baked with layers of butter and sugar. The result is a caramelized and crispy exterior with a tender, buttery interior, offering a unique blend of sweetness and texture.

Kwasong

Kwasong is a Korean dessert made from glutinous rice flour. It is shaped into small balls, filled with sweet ingredients such as red bean paste or sesame seeds, and coated with a powder or syrup. Kwasong is known for its chewy and slightly sticky texture, making it a delightful treat in Korean cuisine.

Pain Chocolate

Pain au chocolat, also known as chocolate croissant, is a French pastry made with flaky and buttery puff pastry dough wrapped around a bar of chocolate. When baked, the dough becomes crisp and golden, and the chocolate inside melts, resulting in a heavenly combination of buttery layers and rich chocolate.

Scones

Scones are a traditional British baked treat that resemble slightly sweet biscuits. They are made with flour, butter, sugar, and sometimes add-ins like dried fruits, chocolate chips, or nuts. Scones have a crumbly texture and are often served with clotted cream and jam, making them a delightful option for afternoon tea.
